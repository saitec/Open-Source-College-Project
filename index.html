College Seminars

Every semester, multiple times, as an Indian engineering undergrad we have had to spend a lot of hours making reports, slides and seminars of often repetitive topics. This sort of teacher-pleasing, time-draining work is probably the thing that we could all live without.

Let's fix it?

I plan on making my collective work freely accessible to anybody who wishes to use it.

What's in the whole thing?

  • My entire archive of Seminars, Reports and resources used over 8 semesters.
  • Research material that includes about 5000 odd published papers
  • The rest is pretty self explanatory
